**When**: **During execution** (not a separate phase)

- Quality control

- Measure customer satisfaction
- Archive documents
- Release resources

## ğŸ” Difference Between Terms

### Customer vs Sponsor vs Owner:

#### Customer/Client:
- **True owner** of the project
- **Owner** of the project
- Who will use or benefit from the project

**Example**: 
- School building project
- **Customer** = Ministry of Education (project owner)

---

#### Sponsor:
- **Funder** of the project
- Who provides **money**
- May be **same** as customer or **external entity**

**Example 1**: 
- School project
- **Sponsor** = International organization funding project (e.g., UN)
- **Customer** = Ministry of Education (project owner)

**Example 2**:
- Power plant project
- **Customer** = Ministry of Electricity (owner)
- **Sponsor** = International bank providing loan
