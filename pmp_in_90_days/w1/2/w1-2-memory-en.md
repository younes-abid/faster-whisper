## ðŸŽ¯ Core Definitions - Memorize Exactly

### Project
> A **temporary endeavor** to create a **unique product, service, or result**

**Characteristics**:
- Temporary
- Unique
- Defined beginning and end

---

### Program
> A group of **related projects** managed in a coordinated way to obtain **benefits**

**Characteristics**:
- Contains **related** projects
- Projects are interconnected
- Share: objectives, resources, budget

---

### Portfolio
> A collection of **programs, projects, and operations** managed to achieve **strategic objectives**

**Characteristics**:
- May contain **independent** projects
- Aligned with strategic objectives
- May include operational work
