## ğŸ¢ Functional Organization

**Definition**: Traditional structure divided into departments (HR, IT, Finance...)

**Project Manager**: 
- **No or very little** authority
- May be called **Coordinator**

**Staff**: 
- Report to Functional Manager
- Part-time on projects

## ğŸš€ Projectized Organization

**Definition**: **Entire company** operates on projects

**Project Manager**: 
- **Full authority** (85-100%)

**Staff**: 
- **Fully dedicated** (Full-time)

## ğŸ”€ Matrix Organizations

### Weak Matrix

- **Close to** Functional
- PM = Coordinator only
- **Limited** authority (25-40%)
- Staff report to Functional Manager

### Balanced Matrix

- **Middle ground** between Weak and Strong
- PM has **moderate** authority (40-60%)
- Staff report to **both**
- **PMO** coordinates resources

### Strong Matrix

- **Close to** Projectized
- PM has **high** authority (60-85%)
- Staff report to PM
- Functional Managers = **advisory only**
