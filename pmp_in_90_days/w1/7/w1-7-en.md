#### 8. Sequence Activities

**Goal**: Logically arrange activities

#### 9. Estimate Activity Durations

**Goal**: How long will each activity take?

**Depends on**:
- Number of resources
- Type of equipment
- Nature of work

**Practical Example**:

**Activity: Excavate Foundations**

- **Scenario 1**: 5 workers + 2 excavators = **2 days**
- **Scenario 2**: 2 workers + 1 excavator = **5 days**

**Influencing Factors**:
- Number of workers
- Soil type (rocky? sandy?)
- Size of excavators
- Daily working hours

---

#### 10. Develop Schedule

**Goal**: Create project **Calendar**

**Output**: 
- Detailed schedule
- Start and end dates
- Milestones

### Phase 4: Cost Planning

#### 11. Estimate Costs

**Goal**: How much will each activity cost?

**Includes**:
- Labor wages
- Material costs
- Equipment costs
- Administrative costs

---

#### 12. Determine Budget

**Goal**: Total budget + **reserves**

**Includes**:
- Sum of estimated costs
- Contingency Reserve
- Management Reserve

---

### Phase 5: Risk Management

#### 13. Identify Risks

**Goal**: What things could go wrong?

**Tools**:
- Brainstorming
- SWOT Analysis
- Review of previous projects

---

#### 14. Perform Qualitative Risk Analysis

**Goal**: Classify risks by:
- **Probability**
- **Impact**

**Example**:
- High risk: high probability + major impact
- Low risk: low probability + minor impact

---

#### 15. Perform Quantitative Risk Analysis

**Goal**: How much will this risk cost in numbers?

**Example**:
- Risk of supplier delay = **$10,000** potential loss
- Risk of material price increase = **5 days** additional delay

---

#### 16. Plan Risk Responses

**Goal**: How will we deal with each risk?

**Eight Strategies**:

**For Negative Risks (Threats)**:
1. **Avoid**
2. **Transfer**
3. **Mitigate**
4. **Accept**

**For Positive Risks (Opportunities)**:
5. **Exploit**
6. **Share**
7. **Enhance**
8. **Accept**
